## Strategy

- **Ordered by dependency**: early packets unblock or reveal later ones.
- **Early packets high-value low-risk** (build confidence, verify build pipeline).
- **Invariant foundations first** (money ledger, cap guards) before architectural rebuilds.
- **Architectural rebuilds before balance tuning** (no point tuning broken feedback loops).

## Execution policy

- One packet per working session.
